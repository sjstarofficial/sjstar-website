About SJ Star

SJ Star is a fast-rising Nigerian Afrobeats artist known for his infectious melodies, heartfelt lyrics, and energetic sound. With releases like Melanin Vibe, Bad For Me, Jeje Love, and many more, SJ Star is building a growing fanbase through authentic storytelling and vibrant Afrobeats rhythms. His mission is to create timeless music that connects people across the world.
